  My name is Matthew Lungu, and I hail from the charming town of Wilmette, Illinois, just a stone's throw away from the bustling city of Chicago. Growing up, I embraced an active lifestyle, attending New Trier High School, where I participated in a variety of sports before discovering my passion for running. This love for athletics carried me into college, where I proudly continued my journey as a track athlete at Lake Forest College.

  Currently, I am pursuing a major in computer science, a field that captivates my curiosity and aligns perfectly with my knack for problem-solving. This past summer, I took the entrepreneurial plunge and teamed up with friends to start a business aimed at assisting our local neighbors with handyman tasks, fostering a sense of community and practicality.

  When I’m not hitting the books or managing our business, you can find me indulging in my favorite hobbies—playing video games and working out. Both activities allow me to unwind and maintain a balanced lifestyle, fueling my passion for both technology and physical fitness. As I navigate this exciting journey, I look forward to combining my interests and making a positive impact in my community.
